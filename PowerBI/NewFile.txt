Microsoft Power BI is a powerful business intelligence and data visualization tool developed by Microsoft. It helps organizations analyze data and make better business decisions.
Power BI allows users to connect data from multiple sources such as Excel, SQL databases, and cloud services. It also helps in transforming and cleaning data before analysis.
Using Power BI, users can create interactive dashboards and reports with charts, graphs, maps, tables, and KPI cards. These visualizations make complex data easier to understand and interpret.
Power BI is widely used for sales analysis, financial reporting, marketing analytics, and performance tracking in many industries. One of its major advantages is its simple drag-and-drop interface, which allows even beginners to create professional reports easily.
It also supports real-time data monitoring and cloud sharing, making collaboration and decision-making faster and more effective within organizations.
